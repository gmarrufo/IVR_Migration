Action required: Resetting your Unified Messaging password

Need to reset your Unified Messaging password? Follow these simple instructions

Audience:

Recent callers to the IBM Help Desk.

 

Action required:

Resetting your Unified Messaging password is easy, and it doesn't require Help Desk assistance. Save and follow these instructions whenever you need to reset your Unified Messaging password.

 

Instructions:

1.    Dial your local Unified Messaging access number.

  If you are unsure of the number, please visit the Unified Messaging Local Access site for a listing of locations and access numbers.

2.    Visit Unified Messaging password reset for instructions.

Notes:

•        If you enter an invalid serial number, you can try again up to 3 times. If your third attempt is incorrect, you will be advised that the maximum number of attempts has been reached, and you will be transferred to the IBM Help Desk. If you have forgotten your serial number, please contact your manager.

•        If you change your password more frequently than every 90 days, you will not be able to use the same password for a period of 365 days.
